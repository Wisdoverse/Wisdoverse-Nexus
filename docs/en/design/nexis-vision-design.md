# Wisdoverse Nexus Vision Design

This document records product direction for an AI-native collaboration platform
combining messaging, documents, meetings, and AI agents. It is directional and
does not describe a complete shipped product surface.

Key principles:

- AI and human members share the same collaboration model.
- Core capabilities are composable across chat, docs, and workflows.
- Governance capabilities should be designed around tenant isolation, audit
  trails, and explicit policy boundaries.
- Extensibility is achieved through skills and A2A protocols.
